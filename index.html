Immigration Notes

Minas Gerais attracts internal migrants for mining and agriculture jobs. International for specialized mining roles. Immigration cases include skilled worker visas. Challenges include environmental impacts from mining. Opportunities in food exports and cultural tourism.

  • tourism (colonial sites)
  • technology

Demographics

Population ~21 million; historical European immigration; diverse.

Industrial growth; wine and cheese exports.
